How We Work

Always Free for Families. Agencies Pay Us.

Searching, browsing, and requesting matches is 100% free — forever. We earn a referral fee from the agency only when your family is successfully placed in care. No catch, no hidden cost, no obligation.

🤝
How We Make Money (We Believe in Transparency)

When your family connects with a provider through Steadfast and care begins, the agency pays us a referral fee — typically 10–15% of the first month's care cost. Your family never pays it. This is the same model used by every major care referral service in the country. We tell you because you deserve to know.

Privacy Policy

Effective June 28, 2026 · Steadfast Senior Referrals · Massachusetts

What Information We Collect

When you submit an inquiry, search for providers, or apply as a provider, we may collect:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Location and region within Massachusetts
  • Care needs, service preferences, and timeline
  • Provider business details (for provider applicants)
  • Any additional details you voluntarily provide

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Match families with appropriate senior care providers
  • Respond to inquiries and referral requests
  • Process and review provider applications
  • Send relevant follow-up communications
  • Maintain internal records for referral tracking

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services We Use

  • Formspree (formspree.io) — receives form submissions from this website
  • Stripe (stripe.com) — processes premium membership payments securely
  • GitHub Pages — hosts this website (no user data stored by GitHub)
  • Gmail / Google — our business email for follow-up communications

Data Security

This website is served over HTTPS (encrypted in transit). We maintain a Written Information Security Program in compliance with Massachusetts 201 CMR 17.00. We do not store payment card numbers — all payments are processed by Stripe.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time. Contact us at the information below.
